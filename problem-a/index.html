Tyler Durden

Tyler Durden is a fictional character and the main antagonist and anti hero of the 1996 Chuck Palahniuk novel Fight Club and in its 1999 cult film adaptation of the same name. He is portrayed by the actor Brad Pitt, Who also played in movies such as Inglorious Basterds and 12 Monkeys. Now, what makes Tyler such an iconic villain? Is the fact that he represents ademption of masculinity repackaged as the promise of violence in the interests of social and political anarchy.
